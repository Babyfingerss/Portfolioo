Project 1: E-Commerce Website

Design of a Home Decor Website

Overview

  1. Project Name & Objective:
    • Name: The Dot - Home Decor Website
    • Objective: Design of an engaging, user-friendly, and visually appealing e-commerce platform for home decor products that emphasizes ease of navigation, product discovery, and streamlined purchasing.
  2. Target Audience & End Users:
    • Homeowners, interior designers, or individuals looking to elevate their living spaces with premium decor.

Design Process

  1. Design Challenge and Scope:
    • Challenge: How can we create a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for customers, while highlighting unique decor items?
    • Scope: Homepage, Product Page, Cart and Payment Details
    • Timeline: 5 days
  2. Design Tools Used:
    • Figma
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript

Impact

The "The Dot - Home Decor Website" project successfully created an intuitive, user-friendly e-commerce platform. With a focus on seamless navigation, visually appealing design, and responsive features, it enhances the shopping experience for homeowners and interior designers alike. The well-structured layout, including key sections like "Best Selling Decor" and "New Arrivals," promotes product visibility and user engagement. Overall, this project has set a strong foundation for future enhancements while meeting the needs of its target audience.
